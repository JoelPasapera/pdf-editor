## 📖 Overview

Guillotina PDF is a modern, high-performance PDF editor that runs entirely inside your browser.

Unlike traditional online PDF editors, **your files never leave your computer**. Every operation is performed locally using JavaScript.

Designed to handle both small documents and large PDF collections efficiently through optimized rendering, thumbnail caching and virtualized page management.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📄 PDF Editing

- Open one or multiple PDFs
- Merge PDFs
- Delete pages
- Duplicate pages
- Reorder pages via Drag & Drop
- Multi-page selection
- Rotate pages
- Live page preview

---

### ✂️ Crop Editor

- Crop individual pages
- Crop selected pages
- Crop entire document
- Real-time preview
- Crop by centimeters
- Crop by pixels
- Resize automatically to A4 after cropping

---

### ⚡ Performance

- Lazy rendering
- Thumbnail cache
- Virtualized page list
- Incremental rendering
- Memory cleanup
- Optimized export
- Responsive interface

Designed for large PDFs with hundreds of pages.

---

### 🔒 Privacy

No uploads.

No servers.

No tracking.

Everything happens locally.

## ⭐ Why Guillotina?

Most online PDF editors upload your files to external servers.

Guillotina keeps every document on your own computer, providing:

- Better privacy
- Better performance
- Offline support
- Faster editing
